SSN College of Engineering

Founded by Dr. Shiv Nadar, Chairman, HCL Technologies, SSN Institutions stands out as a premier centre of higher learning with a mission to pursue excellence in education and research. The institution offers students a distinctive combination of the finest Undergraduate, Postgraduate and Research programs. The diverse student fraternity of SSN enjoy world class facilities and a residential campus set on a sprawling 250 acres of sylvan surroundings.

Department of Information Technology

The Department of Information Technology was established in the year 1999. Our mission is to provide quality education to the students and impart IT excellence in them. Apart from making them good technocrats, we also provide individual attention to make them good citizens of our nation to serve the industry and society constructively.

SSN IEEE Student Branch

The IEEE Student Branch of SSN College of Engineering was established in the year 1998. Institute of Electrical and Electronic Engineers, IEEE, is the largest technical and professional organization for the advancement of technology on a global scale. IEEE, has a motivated student branch at SSN, known for organizing innovative events with great zeal. Hack & Tackle, is one of its flagship events.

HACK AND TACKLE 2.0

Hack & Tackle, is an inter and intra-college hackathon. The first edition, conducted from 31st Jan - 1st Feb 2019, was lauded for its efficient logistics despite the grand participation from across the state. Keeping up with its intention to provide an unhindered platform for students to develop realistic and intelligent solutions to problems prevalent in the society, the second edition of this magnificent event will be held on 20 Feb 2020. Striving for the 2020 vision by stimulating the spark in the youth has been the principle motive behind this event. We hope this year’s edition beholds students surpassing expectations and coming out in flying colours.

IDEATION PHASE

Candidates must register for the challenge, and then submit an abstract. A track must be chosen, ie., the discipline under which the problem falls under following which a write-up on the plausible solution is required to be submitted.

24 HOUR HACKATHON

Solutions from the ideation phase are shortlisted and invited to the onsite 24- hour hackathon, at the end of which, teams are expected to deliver a working prototype of the proposed idea.

NOTE : If your solution doesn't fit in the above track, fear not. You can apply with the open track expressing any suitable idea or concept that solves a real - world problem.

How will the event be conducted?

The hackathon will take place in two phases. In phase 1, the participants will upload the solution of the problem statement along with the registration form. The ideas and the solutions can be based on any of the aforementioned tracks. The plausible solutions will be judged based on the scope, novelty, feasibility, technology, utility and social relevance. Then, the best proposals will be chosen for phase 2.
